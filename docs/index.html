Watch More Movies

By Celeste Olivarez

Why Are Movies so Important?

Movies have always been more than just entertainment for me. They’re a way to connect with emotion, memory and perspective. I love the feeling of getting lost in a story, of seeing the world through someone else’s eyes, even if just for a couple hours. Whether it's laughing until I cry or sitting in silence through the credits, movies create moments that stick. And there’s something special about going out to watch them — sitting in a dark theater, surrounded by strangers all experiencing the same thing at the same time. It’s a shared experience that reminds me why storytelling matters. In a world that moves fast, movies slow us down, pull us in, and invite us to feel something real.
